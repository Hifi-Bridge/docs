## Inflows

Inflow APIs help you create entry points for value so funds can move into your system from bank rails and digital assets.

| Product | Description | Use this to |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Virtual Accounts** | Issue unique local account details for supported markets and currencies to receive deposits. | Accept recurring or one-time inbound bank transfers and reconcile funds per user or account. |
| **Onramps** | Convert fiat into supported stablecoin balances through programmatic transfer flows. | Bring user or treasury funds on-chain from local bank rails. |
| **Wallet Deposits** | Provision custodial wallets that can receive and hold supported digital assets. | Let users or your business receive stablecoin deposits into managed custody. |

## Controls

Control APIs give you programmable control over how money moves internally across accounts, wallets, chains, and workflows.

| Product | Description | Use this to |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Transfers** | Trigger one-time transfer operations across supported rails and counterparties. | Move funds programmatically between entities in your product lifecycle. |
| **Wallet Transfers** | Manage digital asset movement between wallets and destinations. | Power treasury, internal rebalancing, and operational crypto movements. |
| **Batch Transfers** | Submit grouped transfer actions in a single orchestration workflow. | Execute high-volume payouts or internal disbursements efficiently. |
| **Bridging** | Move assets across supported chains while keeping workflows unified. | Route liquidity or settle value across multiple networks. |
| **Swaps** | Exchange supported assets to meet settlement or treasury requirements. | Convert one digital asset into another as part of a payment or treasury flow. |
| **Settlement Rules** | Configure deterministic routing logic for incoming funds and account behavior. | Automate value routing and reduce manual operational intervention. |
| **Orchestration Addresses** | A static onchain address that orchestrate the funds flow with flexible configuration | Move and convert funds based on predefined rules |
| **Transfer Approvals** | Add policy-based approval steps before transfer execution. | Enforce internal controls and governance for sensitive fund movement. |

## Outflows

Outflow APIs help you settle to users, partners, and counterparties across bank rails and digital asset destinations.

| Product | Description | Use this to |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Offramps** | Convert stablecoin balances into fiat and deliver to bank payout endpoints. | Settle users or partners in local currency. |
| **Coverage** | Explore supported regions, corridors, and payment rails. | Plan where and how you can settle payments to. |

## Compliance

HIFI includes built-in onboarding and compliance primitives so product teams can launch regulated money movement with fewer integration layers.

| Product | Description | Use this to |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| **Users** | Create and manage end users or business entities in your integration. | Maintain customer records tied to wallet, account, and transfer activity. |
| **KYC/KYB** | Collect and evaluate identity and business verification requirements. | Satisfy onboarding controls before enabling production money movement. |
| **KYC Links** | Generate hosted onboarding flows for compliance submission. | Offload document collection and verification UX while keeping API control. |
| **Token Sharing** | Share your end users KYC information via token provided by eligible compliance vendor | Enable faster onboarding for your end users with less integration overhead |

## Private Transactions

For institution-grade settlement between trusted counterparties, HIFI also supports private transaction workflows designed for controlled, compliant value movement.
