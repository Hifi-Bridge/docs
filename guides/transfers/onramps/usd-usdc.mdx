Learn how to convert fiat currencies to stablecoins using HIFI's onramp services.

## Overview

HIFI's onramp services allow you to convert fiat currencies to stablecoins using local payment methods and bank rails. This enables:

- Fiat to crypto conversions
- Cross-border payments
- Local payment method support
- Competitive exchange rates

## Supported currencies

### Fiat currencies

- **Americas**: USD, CAD, MXN, BRL, ARS, CLP, COP
- **Europe**: EUR, GBP, CHF, SEK, NOK, DKK
- **Asia-Pacific**: JPY, KRW, SGD, HKD, INR, AUD, NZD
- **Middle East & Africa**: AED, SAR, ZAR, EGP

### Stablecoins

- USDC (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Solana)
- USDT (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Solana)
- DAI (Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base)
- BUSD (Ethereum, BSC)

## Payment methods

### Bank transfers

- **ACH** (US) - 1-3 business days
- **SEPA** (EU) - 1-2 business days
- **SWIFT** (International) - 2-5 business days
- **Local bank transfers** - 1-3 business days

### Cards

- **Credit cards** - Instant to 1 hour
- **Debit cards** - Instant to 1 hour
- **Prepaid cards** - Instant to 1 hour

### Digital wallets

- **PayPal** - 5-30 minutes
- **Apple Pay** - Instant to 15 minutes
- **Google Pay** - Instant to 15 minutes
- **Local wallets** - 5-60 minutes

### Local payment methods

- **OXXO** (Mexico) - 1-24 hours
- **Boleto** (Brazil) - 1-24 hours
- **Konbini** (Japan) - 1-24 hours
- **GCash** (Philippines) - 5-30 minutes

## Basic onramp

```javascript
const onramp = await hifi.onramps.create({
  from_currency: "USD",
  to_currency: "USDC",
  amount: "1000.00",
  wallet: "wallet_123",
  payment_method: "bank_transfer",
});
```

## Advanced onramp

```javascript
const onramp = await hifi.onramps.create({
  from_currency: "USD",
  to_currency: "USDC",
  amount: "1000.00",
  wallet: "wallet_123",
  payment_method: "bank_transfer",
  network: "ethereum",
  memo: "Initial deposit",
  metadata: {
    source: "savings_account",
    purpose: "investment",
  },
});
```

## Get quote

```javascript
// Get a quote before converting
const quote = await hifi.onramps.getQuote({
  from_currency: "USD",
  to_currency: "USDC",
  amount: "1000.00",
  payment_method: "bank_transfer",
});

console.log(`Exchange rate: 1 USD = ${quote.rate} USDC`);
console.log(`Expected output: ${quote.expected_output} USDC`);
console.log(`Processing time: ${quote.estimated_time} hours`);
console.log(`Fees: ${quote.fee} USD`);

// Execute the onramp
const onramp = await hifi.onramps.create({
  from_currency: "USD",
  to_currency: "USDC",
  amount: "1000.00",
  wallet: "wallet_123",
  payment_method: "bank_transfer",
  quote_id: quote.id,
});
```

## Fees and limits

### Fees by payment method

- **Bank transfers**: 0.5-1.5% of amount
- **Cards**: 2.5-3.5% of amount
- **Digital wallets**: 1.0-2.0% of amount
- **Local methods**: 1.5-2.5% of amount

### Transaction limits

- **Minimum**: $10 USD equivalent
- **Maximum**: $50,000 USD equivalent per transaction
- **Daily limit**: $100,000 USD equivalent per user
- **Monthly limit**: $500,000 USD equivalent per user

### KYC verification levels

- **Basic**: $1,000 daily limit
- **Enhanced**: $10,000 daily limit
- **Premium**: $50,000 daily limit

## Error handling

Common onramp errors:

- **Insufficient balance**: Check payment method has enough funds
- **KYC required**: Complete identity verification
- **Unsupported currency**: Check supported currency list
- **Payment method declined**: Try alternative payment method
- **Daily limit exceeded**: Wait for next day or upgrade KYC

## Best practices

1. **Complete KYC early** to avoid delays
2. **Check exchange rates** before large conversions
3. **Use appropriate payment methods** for your needs
4. **Keep transaction IDs** for support requests
5. **Monitor processing times** for optimal timing
