Business users from high risk countries are supported for USD payouts through enhanced KYB as part of a pilot program.

<Warning>
  High-risk-country onboarding is part of a pilot program. Contact HIFI before
  enabling USD payouts for customers in these countries.
</Warning>

The business-level document review is the same as standard KYB, but owner verification and payment monitoring are stricter.

## Supported Countries

| Country | ISO Code | Rail | Direction | Payment Method |
| :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- | :-- |
| India | IN | USD | Payout | SWIFT |
| Panama | PA | USD | Payout | SWIFT |
| Philippines | PH | USD | Payout | SWIFT |
| Saudi Arabia | SA | USD | Payout | SWIFT |
| Vietnam | VN | USD | Payout | SWIFT |

<Info>
  High risk countries are not prohibited regions. They require enhanced KYB,
  additional UBO verification, and lower transaction review limits.
</Info>

## What Changes

| Area | Standard KYB | High Risk Country KYB |
| :-- | :-- | :-- |
| Entity documents | Baseline business document checks | No change |
| UBO threshold | UBOs with more than 25% ownership | All UBOs with 10% ownership or above |
| UBO verification | Standard individual KYC for required UBOs | Full individual KYC plus live ID capture and biometric liveness verification |
| Review threshold | Single transactions or payments of $25,000 USD or more | Single transactions or payments of $10,000 USD or more |

<Note>
  The key KYB difference is at the ownership level. Every UBO at or above the
  10% ownership threshold must be individually verified before the entity can be
  approved.
</Note>

## Onboarding Flow

<Steps>
  <Step title="Identify country risk">
    Check whether the business is from one of the supported high risk countries
    before starting KYB.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Collect business information">
    Collect the standard USD rail KYB fields and baseline business documents.
    Entity-level document collection does not change for high risk countries.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add UBOs at 10% ownership or above">
    Include every Ultimate Beneficial Owner with 10% ownership or above, not
    only owners above the standard 25% threshold.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete UBO liveness verification">
    Each qualifying UBO must complete the hosted liveness link, including live ID
    capture and biometric liveness verification.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for KYB approval">
    Enable USD payouts only after the business KYB status is active.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## UBO Verification Requirements

For each UBO at or above 10% ownership:

- Collect the required individual KYC information.
- Require live camera capture for identity document collection.
- Require biometric liveness verification.
- Do not accept static image uploads or screenshots of identity documents.

<Warning>
  Static document uploads are not accepted for high-risk-country UBO identity
  checks. The UBO must complete verification through the liveness link.
</Warning>

## Payment Review Limits

Any single transaction or payment of $10,000 USD or more is held for review before processing.

Build your product and operations workflow so users can see when a payment is pending review and so your team can monitor review outcomes before treating the payment as processed.

## Implementation Checklist

- Add the five high risk countries to your country support logic.
- Route high-risk-country businesses through enhanced KYB.
- Collect all UBOs with 10% ownership or above.
- Send each qualifying UBO through the liveness verification link.
- Wait for active KYB status before enabling USD payouts.
- Hold single transactions or payments of $10,000 USD or more for review before processing.
