Pricing and transaction limits can vary by integration, payment rail, geography, compliance profile, and production approval status. Contact HIFI for account-specific pricing and approved limits.

## Transaction Processing Limits

The following default limits apply to domestic and cross-border transactions unless your agreement or dashboard configuration states otherwise.

### Sender Limits

| Frequency | Domestic Transactions | Cross-Border Transactions | Domestic Amount | Cross-Border Amount |
| :-- | --: | --: | --: | --: |
| Single transaction | 1 | 1 | 15,000 | 15,000 |
| Daily | 30 | 30 | 50,000 | 50,000 |
| Weekly | 50 | 50 | 100,000 | 100,000 |
| Monthly | 150 | 150 | 200,000 | 200,000 |

### Recipient Limits

| Frequency | Domestic Transactions | Cross-Border Transactions | Domestic Amount | Cross-Border Amount |
| :-- | --: | --: | --: | --: |
| Single transaction | 1 | 1 | 15,000 | 15,000 |
| Daily | 30 | 30 | 50,000 | 50,000 |
| Weekly | 50 | 50 | 100,000 | 100,000 |
| Monthly | 150 | 150 | 200,000 | 200,000 |

<Note>
  These tables describe transaction processing limits only. Account-specific
  pricing, currencies, and approved limits may differ by integration.
</Note>
