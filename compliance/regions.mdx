## Prohibited Regions

<Warning>
  HIFI does not support individuals or businesses from the regions below. You will not be able to create Users or Transfers within these jurisdictions.
</Warning>

- Cuba (CU)
- Iran (IR)
- Korea, North (KP)
- Syria (SY)

## High Risk Countries

Some countries are supported through enhanced KYB as high risk countries. These countries are not prohibited, but business users require additional owner verification and payment monitoring before they can transact normally.

Current high risk countries:

| Country | ISO Code |
| :-- | :-- |
| India | IN |
| Panama | PA |
| Philippines | PH |
| Saudi Arabia | SA |
| Vietnam | VN |

<Info>
  See the [High Risk Countries guide](/guides/high-risk-countries) for enhanced
  KYB requirements, UBO verification, and payment review limits.
</Info>
