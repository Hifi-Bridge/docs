Unlocking a **Rail** enables a user's access to specific payment networks and geographic regions. Each rail provides a specialized connection to specific markets and payment methods, enabling pay-ins (onramps) and pay-outs (offramps) for users and businesses.

## Available Rails
HIFI supports four rails:

- **USD Rail** – enables payins and payouts to the US
- **Global Network Rail** – enables payouts to multiple regions
- **Africa Rail** – enables payins and payouts to multiple countries
- **Fiat Account Rail** – enables custodial fiat accounts for holding and sending funds

## USD Rail

The USD Rail enables USD pay-ins and pay-outs through multiple payment methods and supports virtual accounts for onramps.

**Payment Methods:**

- ACH (Automated Clearing House)
- RTP (Real-Time Payments)
- Wire transfers

**Features:**

- Virtual accounts for onramps
- Payouts include user's KYC/B name
- Full USD onramp and offramp capabilities

Learn more: [USD Rail](/docs/rails/usd)

## Global Network Rail

The Global Network Rail enables payouts to Asia and Africa with support for multiple currencies.

**Supported Regions:**

- **China (CN)**: USD and CNY
- **Hong Kong (HK)**: USD and HKD
- **Other regions**: BRL, MXN, NGN

Learn more: [Global Network Rail](/docs/rails/global-network)

## Africa Rail

The Africa Rail enables payins and payouts to various African countries with local currency support.

**Supported Countries:**

- Benin (BEN)
- Botswana (BWA)
- Burkina Faso (BFA)
- Cameroon (CMR)
- Côte d'Ivoire (CIV)
- Kenya (KEN)
- Malawi (MWI)
- Mali (MLI)
- Nigeria (NGA)
- Senegal (SEN)
- South Africa (ZAF)
- Tanzania (TZA)
- Togo (TGO)
- Uganda (UGA)
- Zambia (ZMB)

Learn more: [Africa Rail](/docs/rails/africa)

## Fiat Account Rail

The Fiat Account Rail (`fiat_account`) enables custodial fiat accounts for end users. Users can deposit fiat currency via SWIFT, hold balances, and send payouts to beneficiaries.

**Payment Methods:**

- SWIFT (deposits and payouts)

**Features:**

- Custodial fiat account holding
- SWIFT deposits and payouts
- Domestic (US) payouts (coming soon)
- Stablecoin conversion (in development)

Learn more: [Fiat Accounts](/docs/accounts/fiat-accounts)
