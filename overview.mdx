## What you can do

- **Send and receive payments:** Move money between fiat and stablecoins across borders through a single API.
- **Create virtual accounts:** Issue deposit accounts for users in supported currencies to manage deposits.
- **Provision wallets:** Generate custodial wallets for users or businesses to hold and transfer funds securely.
- **Manage on-chain assets:** Transfer or swap digital assets between chains using on-chain operations.
- **Automate fund flows:** Use APIs to manage internal transfers, settlements, and liquidity automatically.

## Common use cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Payments">
    Build comprehensive payment solutions that enable seamless money movement across borders and currencies.

    **Cross-border payments**

    Enable users to send money internationally through stablecoins with instant settlement. Convert fiat to USDC (onramp), transfer across borders instantly without traditional banking delays, and convert back to local currency (offramp). Leverage HIFI's global network to reach recipients in multiple countries while maintaining low fees and transparent exchange rates.

    **Remittances**

    Build remittance products for users sending money home to family and friends. Support multiple currencies and geographic regions through our extensive network of offramp partners. Provide competitive exchange rates, fast settlement times, and transparent fee structures. Integrate KYC links and compliance workflows to ensure regulatory adherence across different jurisdictions.

    **Bill payments**

    Facilitate automated bill payment services for utilities, subscriptions, and other recurring expenses. Use virtual accounts to collect payments from users and route funds to billers automatically. Support batch transfers to process multiple payments efficiently, reducing operational overhead while ensuring timely settlements.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Marketplaces">
    Power marketplace platforms with flexible payment infrastructure and automated fee distribution.

    **Marketplace payments**

    Enable crypto-native payments in your marketplace platform. Allow users to onramp fiat currency, make purchases using stablecoins, and offramp earnings back to their bank accounts. Support escrow functionality using HIFI's transfer approval workflows to hold funds until transactions are confirmed. Provide multi-currency support so buyers and sellers can transact in their preferred currencies.

    **Platform fee splits**

    Automate fee collection and distribution through settlement rules and batch transfer capabilities. Configure automatic splits where a percentage of each transaction flows to your platform while the remainder goes to sellers. Use developer fees to seamlessly collect platform revenue without manual reconciliation. Support complex payout structures with multiple recipients per transaction.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fintech">
    Create innovative fintech applications with digital wallets, peer-to-peer transfers, and comprehensive financial infrastructure.

    **Digital wallet**

    Provision custodial wallets for users or businesses to hold and transfer funds securely. Support multiple currencies including stablecoins and fiat across HIFI's virtual account network. Enable deposits, withdrawals, and internal transfers through a unified API. Integrate balance management and transaction history to give users full visibility into their financial activity. Leverage HIFI's compliance infrastructure to ensure regulatory requirements are met automatically.

    **P2P transfers**

    Build peer-to-peer transfer capabilities that allow users to send money to each other instantly. Support transfers between users within your platform, across different currencies, and to external recipients. Use virtual accounts to enable seamless deposits and withdrawals. Implement transfer approval workflows for added security and control. Support batch transfers for bulk operations and provide real-time notifications through webhooks.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
