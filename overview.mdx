## Inflows

Inflow APIs help you create entry points for value so funds can move into your system from bank rails and digital assets.

| Product                                            | Description                                                                                  | Use this to                                                                                  |
| :------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[Virtual Accounts](/accounts/virtual-accounts)** | Issue unique local account details for supported markets and currencies to receive deposits. | Accept recurring or one-time inbound bank transfers and reconcile funds per user or account. |
| **[Onramps](/transactions/onramps)**               | Convert fiat into supported stablecoin balances through programmatic transfer flows.         | Bring user or treasury funds on-chain from local bank rails.                                 |
| **[Wallet Deposits](/wallets)**                    | Provision custodial wallets that can receive and hold supported digital assets.              | Let users or your business receive stablecoin deposits into managed custody.                 |

---

## Management

Management APIs give you programmable control over how money moves internally across accounts, wallets, chains, and workflows.

| Product                                                | Description                                                                     | Use this to                                                                   |
| :----------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------------ | :---------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[Transfers](/transactions/transfers)**               | Trigger one-time transfer operations across supported rails and counterparties. | Move funds programmatically between entities in your product lifecycle.       |
| **[Wallet Transfers](/transactions/overview)**         | Manage digital asset movement between wallets and destinations.                 | Power treasury, internal rebalancing, and operational crypto movements.       |
| **[Bridging](/transactions/bridging)**                 | Move assets across supported chains while keeping workflows unified.            | Route liquidity or settle value across multiple networks.                     |
| **[Swaps](/transactions/swaps)**                       | Exchange supported assets to meet settlement or treasury requirements.          | Convert one digital asset into another as part of a payment or treasury flow. |
| **[Settlement Rules](/features/settlement-rules)**     | Configure deterministic routing logic for incoming funds and account behavior.  | Automate value routing and reduce manual operational intervention.            |
| **[Batch Transfers](/features/batch-transfers)**       | Submit grouped transfer actions in a single orchestration workflow.             | Execute high-volume payouts or internal disbursements efficiently.            |
| **[Transfer Approvals](/features/transfer-approvals)** | Add policy-based approval steps before transfer execution.                      | Enforce internal controls and governance for sensitive fund movement.         |

---

## Outflows

Outflow APIs help you settle to users, partners, and counterparties across bank rails and digital asset destinations.

| Product                                | Description                                                                 | Use this to                                    |
| :------------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------------- | :--------------------------------------------- |
| **[Offramps](/transactions/offramps)** | Convert stablecoin balances into fiat and deliver to bank payout endpoints. | Settle users or partners in local currency.    |
| **[Coverage](/coverage)**              | Explore supported regions, corridors, and payment rails.                    | Plan where and how you can settle payments to. |

## Compliance

HIFI includes built-in onboarding and compliance primitives so product teams can launch regulated money movement with fewer integration layers.

| Product                              | Description                                                           | Use this to                                                                |
| :----------------------------------- | :-------------------------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **[Users](/users/overview)**         | Create and manage end users or business entities in your integration. | Maintain customer records tied to wallet, account, and transfer activity.  |
| **[KYC/KYB](/kyc)**                  | Collect and evaluate identity and business verification requirements. | Satisfy onboarding controls before enabling production money movement.     |
| **[KYC Links](/features/kyc-links)** | Generate hosted onboarding flows for compliance submission.           | Offload document collection and verification UX while keeping API control. |
