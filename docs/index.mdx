## What you can do

- **Send and receive payments:**
  Move money between fiat and stablecoins across borders through a single API.
- **Create virtual accounts:**
  Issue deposit accounts for users in supported currencies to manage deposits and withdrawals.
- **Provision wallets:**
  Generate custodial wallets for users or businesses to hold and transfer funds securely.
- **Bridge on-chain assets:**
  Transfer or swap digital assets between chains using quote-based or non-quote-based on-chain operations.
- **Automate fund flows:**
  Use APIs to manage internal transfers, settlements, and liquidity automatically.

## Common use cases

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Cross-Border Payments">
    Enable users to send money internationally through stablecoins. Convert fiat
    to USDC, transfer across borders instantly, and offramp to local currency.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Treasury Automation">
    Automate business treasury operations with settlement rules and batch
    transfers. Route incoming deposits automatically and distribute funds to
    multiple accounts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Remittances">
    Build remittance products for users sending money home. Support multiple
    currencies and geographic regions.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Payroll & Payouts">
    Distribute payroll or contractor payments efficiently. Send payments to
    multiple recipients with a single API integration, reducing costs and
    complexity.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Marketplaces">
    Enable crypto-native payments in marketplaces. Allow users to onramp fiat,
    make purchases, and offramp earnings back to their bank accounts.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="DeFi Integration">
    Connect traditional finance with DeFi protocols. Users can onramp fiat,
    interact with DeFi applications, and offramp profits back to traditional
    banking.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Fintech Applications">
    Build comprehensive fintech products with multi-currency support, virtual
    accounts, and automated compliance. Focus on product features while HIFI
    handles infrastructure.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
