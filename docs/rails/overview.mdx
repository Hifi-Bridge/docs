HIFI supports four rails:

- **USD Rail** – enables payins and payouts to the US
- **Global Network Rail** – enables payouts to Asia and Africa
- **Africa Rail** – enables payouts to African countries
- **South America Rail** – enables payins from South American countries

## Details

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="USD">
    The USD Rail enables USD pay-ins and pay-outs through multiple payment methods and supports virtual accounts for onramps.

    **Payment Methods:**
    - ACH (Automated Clearing House)
    - RTP (Real-Time Payments)
    - Wire transfers

    **Features:**
    - Virtual accounts for onramps
    - Payouts include user's KYC/B name
    - Full USD onramp and offramp capabilities

    **Compliance Requirements:**
    - Individual: KYC verification, Source of Funds questionnaire, identity documents
    - Business: KYB verification, UBO information, business registration documents

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Global Network">
    The Global Network Rail enables payouts to Asia and Africa with support for multiple currencies.

    **Supported Regions:**
    - **China (CN)**: USD and CNY
    - **Hong Kong (HK)**: USD and HKD
    - **Other regions**: BRL, MXN, NGN

    **Compliance:**
    - Standardized compliance requirements
    - Multi-currency support
    - Cross-border transaction capabilities

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Africa">
    The Africa Rail enables payouts to various African countries with local currency support.

    **Supported Currencies:**
    - BWP (Botswana Pula)
    - GHS (Ghanaian Cedi)
    - KES (Kenyan Shilling)
    - MWK (Malawian Kwacha)
    - NGN (Nigerian Naira)
    - RWF (Rwandan Franc)
    - TZS (Tanzanian Shilling)
    - UGX (Ugandan Shilling)
    - XAF (Central African CFA Franc)
    - XOF (West African CFA Franc)
    - ZAR (South African Rand)
    - ZMW (Zambian Kwacha)

    **Compliance:**
    - Pan-African compliance standards
    - Regional regulatory requirements
    - Mobile money integration

  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="South America">
    The South America Rail enables payins from South American countries with local currency support.

    **Supported Currencies:**
    - ARS (Argentine Peso)
    - BRL (Brazilian Real)
    - COP (Colombian Peso)
    - MXN (Mexican Peso)

    **Compliance:**
    - Region-specific compliance requirements
    - Local regulatory standards
    - South American banking partnerships

  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
