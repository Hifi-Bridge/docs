sidebarTitle: "Account Purposes"

| Purpose                                          | Description                                                                                                                                                                                                      | Acceptable value                       |
| ------------------------------------------------ | ---------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------------------------- |
| Pay suppliers                                    | Businesses that need to pay their suppliers for goods or services they purchase.                                                                                                                                 | `PAY_SUPPLIERS`                        |
| Pay service providers                            | Businesses that make payments to contractors or service providers (like consultants, agencies, or maintenance services).                                                                                         | `PAY_SERVICE_PROVIDERS`                |
| Pay for goods / merchandise / commodities        | Businesses engaged in purchasing or selling physical goods, merchandise, or commodities.                                                                                                                         | `GOODS_MERCHANDISE_COMMODITIES`        |
| Pay freelancers/contractors/payroll              | Businesses paying employees, freelancers, or contractors for their work.                                                                                                                                         | `FREELANCERS_CONTRACTORS_PAYROLL`      |
| Pay for financial services / loans / investments | Businesses offering financial services, loans, or investment opportunities.                                                                                                                                      | `FINANCIAL_SERVICES_LOANS_INVESTMENTS` |
| Pay taxes or regulatory fees                     | This purpose applies when the business needs to pay taxes or regulatory fees to government agencies.                                                                                                             | `TAX_REGULATORY_FEES`                  |
| Pay for legal services                           | Businesses or individuals paying for legal services.                                                                                                                                                             | `LEGAL_SERVICES`                       |
| Pay for operational expenses                     | Businesses paying general expenses necessary for day-to-day operations, such as utilities, rent, or office supplies.                                                                                             | `OPERATIONAL_EXPENSES`                 |
| Pay subscriptions                                | Applies to businesses paying recurring payments for subscriptions (e.g., software as a service, memberships, or media subscriptions).                                                                            | `SUBSCRIPTIONS`                        |
| Pay for travel related expenses                  | For businesses paying for travel services (flights, hotels, transportation, etc.).                                                                                                                               | `TRAVEL_RELATED_EXPENSES`              |
| Pay for insurance                                | For businesses paying insurance premiums or dealing with insurance-related transactions.                                                                                                                         | `INSURANCE`                            |
| Pay advertising and marketing expenses           | This category involves businesses paying for advertising or marketing services, such as digital campaigns, print ads, partnerships, etc.                                                                         | `ADVERTISING_MARKETING_EXPENSES`       |
| Pay for equipment or machinery                   | Businesses making payments involving the purchase or lease of equipment or machinery used in business operations.                                                                                                | `EQUIPMENT_MACHINERY`                  |
| Pay for technology services                      | Businesses making payments related to software development, IT services, or cloud computing services.                                                                                                            | `TECHNOLOGY_SERVICES`                  |
| Pay for education related expenses               | Businesses making payments related to education services, such as tuition fees, courses, or training programs.                                                                                                   | `EDUCATION_RELATED_EXPENSES`           |
| Pay for treasury payments                        | Businesses making payments involving the movement of funds within a company’s treasury, including inter-company transfers or liquidity management.                                                               | `TREASURY_PAYMENTS`                    |
| Pay for FX services                              | Businesses making payments that involve conducting foreign exchange transactions or currency conversions.                                                                                                        | `FX_SERVICES`                          |
| Other                                            | For business purposes that don't fit the other categories. If a business purpose doesn’t fall under the defined categories, it can be classified as “Other” and still undergo the KYC process for due diligence. | `OTHER`                                |
| Collect from online market places                | Businesses that collect funds or payments from online marketplaces (e.g., e-commerce platforms like Amazon, eBay, etc.).                                                                                         | `ONLINE_MARKETPLACE_TRADING`           |
| Collect from offline trade                       | Businesses that engage in offline trade (e.g., retail transactions, in-person sales, or traditional markets).                                                                                                    | `OFFLINE_TRADING`                      |
