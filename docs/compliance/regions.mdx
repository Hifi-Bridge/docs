HIFI operates globally with services available to individuals and businesses in over 150 countries and regions. However, due to regulatory requirements, certain jurisdictions are restricted or prohibited from using our services.

## Prohibited Regions

<Info>
  HIFI does not support individuals or businesses from the regions below. You
  will not be able to create Users or Transfers within these jurisdictions.
</Info>

- Algeria
- Bangladesh
- Ethiopia
- Eritrea
- Kosovo
- Morocco
- Niger
- Pakistan
- Qatar
- Slovenia
