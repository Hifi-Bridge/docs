title: "Accounts"
---

An onramp or offramp account represents a sender or recipient for fiat operations.

## Supported Accounts

- **US**: ACH, Wire, SWIFT
- **Brazil**: PIX and local payment methods
- **Colombia**: Local payment systems
- **Argentina**: Local payment methods
- **Mexico**: SPEI and local payment systems
- **Africa**: Regional payment methods
- **Global Network**: Brazil, Nigeria, Mexico, Hong Kong, and China
