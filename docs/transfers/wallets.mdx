Wallet transfers are the easiest way to send money on HIFI by transferring funds between wallets on the same blockchain network.

## How Wallet Transfers Work

Wallet transfers allow you to send stablecoins directly from one wallet to another on the same blockchain. This is the most straightforward way to move funds within the HIFI ecosystem.
