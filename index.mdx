HIFI is a developer platform to build financial infrastructure with stablecoins. We provide programmable primitives that give you full control over how money is issued, routed, and reconciled. We've put this guide together to help you get started quickly.

### What you can do with HIFI

- **Convert** – Move money from fiat accounts into stablecoin wallets
- **Route** – Move stablecoins between wallets and chains
- **Send** – Pay out from stablecoins to fiat accounts
- **Screen** – Verify and monitor users and transactions
